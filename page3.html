Hiking

Hiking is a wonderful way to explore nature, get some exercise, and spend time outdoors with friends and family.

I enjoy photographing mushrooms and waterfalls, finding berries to eat and just breathing fresh mountain air.

Fire Dancing

Fire dancing is an art form where performers manipulate torch-like fire props like poi, fans, and staves to create mesmerizing visual displays.

Sword Fighting

Sword fighting is a martial art that involves the use of swords for self-defense, sport, or performance.

The style of sword fighting that I do is SCA rapier fighting. I began practicing in 2022 and try to get to practice once a week in the summer.

There is a tournament held locally every September, and I won it my third year competing.
